About Me

I'm Taras Hrytskiv — a barber, educator and mentor from Ukraine with over 9 years of experience in the barber industry.

My professional journey began in Ukraine, where I became a co-owner of Billy Blade Barbershop and participated in the development of modern barber education, events and professional culture.

Over the years, I helped organize barber masterclasses and industry events attended by hundreds of barbers, focusing on service, communication, branding and long-term professional growth.

After moving to the United States, I continued building my career independently in Los Angeles while mentoring Ukrainian barbers who want to grow professionally in the U.S. market.

Today, I focus on barber education, mentorship and professional development through my own educational system — "The Method of Successful Barber" — a methodology based on real industry experience, client service, communication and personal branding.

I believe modern barbering is not only technical skill, but also professionalism, trust, education and long-term impact.
